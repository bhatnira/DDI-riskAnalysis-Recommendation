Why is warfarin-aspirin a concern?
Warfarin-Aspirin Interaction:

Both medications affect blood clotting through different mechanisms:
  • Warfarin inhibits vitamin K-dependent clotting factors
  • Aspirin inhibits platelet aggregation

Combined, they significantly increase bleeding risk. Monitor for signs of bleeding and maintain INR within target range.
What alternatives should I consider?
Based on the analysis, consider these alternatives:

For Warfarin: Direct oral anticoagulants (DOACs) like apixaban may have fewer drug interactions.

For Aspirin: If used for cardiovascular protection, evaluate if the benefit outweighs the bleeding risk with warfarin.
