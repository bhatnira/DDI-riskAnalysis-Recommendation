\subsection*{DrugBank DDI Dataset: Cardiovascular and Antithrombotic Focus}

The drug--drug interaction (DDI) dataset was extracted from DrugBank, filtered to include interactions involving cardiovascular (ATC class C) or antithrombotic (ATC class B01) agents. The resulting dataset comprises \textbf{759,774 unique DDI pairs} involving \textbf{4,314 distinct drugs} identified by their DrugBank accession numbers. Each interaction record includes the interacting drug pair and a textual description of the interaction mechanism. The therapeutic distribution spans all major ATC classes, with nervous system drugs (N) comprising the largest category at 11.3\%, followed by antineoplastic/immunomodulating agents (L) at 10.5\%, and cardiovascular drugs (C) at 7.9\%.
